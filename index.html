How it works

From idea to URL in four steps.

No setup. No templates to pick from. No code to copy-paste. Clay interviews you like a thoughtful designer would — then ships the site for you.

1

Describe your idea

Tell Clay what you're building in one sentence. "A bakery in Brooklyn." "A yoga studio." That's enough to start.

2

Answer questions

Clay asks a handful of simple design questions — navigation, sections, tone, colors. Pick from options or write your own.

3

Generate the site

Three AI models work together to build a complete website with a design system tuned to your brand.

4

Publish in 60 seconds

Hit publish. Clay pushes your site to GitHub Pages and hands you a live URL. You're online.
