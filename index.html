— How it works

From idea to URL in four moves.

No setup. No templates to pick from. No code to copy-paste. Clay interviews you like a thoughtful designer would — then ships the site for you.

STEP 01

Describe your idea

Tell Clay what you're building in one sentence. "A bakery in Brooklyn." "A yoga studio." That's enough to start.

STEP 02

Answer the questions

Clay asks a handful of fundamental design questions — navigation style, sections, tone, palette. Pick from options or write your own.

STEP 03

Generate the code

Three AI models collaborate to build a complete Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS site, with a design system tuned to your brand.

STEP 04

Publish in 60 seconds

Hit publish. Clay pushes your site to GitHub Pages and hands you a live URL. That's it. You're online.
